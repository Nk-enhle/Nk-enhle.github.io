Portfolio

Dave Mela

I collaborated with the very talented UX Designer and Art Director Dave Mela to craft his new portfolio website. We quickly agreed that his online portfolio should be a combination of simplicity, blocking colors, beautiful interactions and the integration of Dave’s lettering artworks.

One-page layout with a seamless case study integration

Dave’s idea to serve a one-page layout with a seamless integration of her work faced us with the challenge to preserve access to other projects while viewing a project case study in detail. After sketching different ideas the most feasible ones we finally came up with an approach to inject project case studies without loosing the red line and preserve the overall simplicity of the site.

From head to toe – Accessible on all devices

Serving the site on all devices was achieved by using an early version of the open-source framework.You can compare both devices and scroll through the different sections of the site.

SVG Animation Madness

Our vision of the website included the usage of Daves lettering artwork for headlines. We had the idea of animating these headlines and after researching for various possibilities I decided to use SVGs to maintain the overall scaleability of the site and serve high quality animations with just a few kilobytes in size. There seems to be a pretty thin line to success or totally fail by the complexity and sensibility of the SVGs data structure that needs to be created by hand in order to turn the lettering artwork into performant animations. We choose an agile approach to settle the requirements for the file structure and anatomy of the SVGs. While I turned the letters into suitable SVGs first and then applied animations, Dave fine tuned the compositions.

While we both have been coming across some great line animations on websites already, we were pretty stoked by the outcome of our very first rough version. Fine-tuning the timing and overall transition durations took plenty of time but from that point on we knew that every bit we are adding will be totally worth it.
