Farm Gardens
Web-image creation for agrarian company
See more
Aim
Farm Gardens

Create a non-trivial website for agribusiness. Give "added value" to the B2B segment, tell in details about the stages of harvesting berries, the period of maturation and key characteristics of varieties. Pass the image of a young company that creates a benchmark in its niche product and is rapidly gaining momentum.

Client

Farm Gardens

Type of works

Website
Admin-panel

fg
Intro
Situation

Powerful agribusiness in the digital environment often looks naive, helpless and hopelessly behind the times. The idea to invest in the formation of an online image for farmers may seem inappropriate, untimely, if not completely useless; because the harvest is collected and processed in real life, not virtual. But the global tendencies of digitalization of absolutely all spheres of human activity are already very difficult to deny or ignore.

Customer
Farm Gardens

Young dynamic company Farms Gardens; this is a team of like-minded people and 155 hectares of plantations of ecological berries in Ukraine, the quality of which is confirmed by a certificate «Organic Standard».

Site
Concept

The basis of the visual style was the laconic color palette of berry shades, a lot of clean space and mouth-watering photos. Much attention is paid to rethinking the information in order to present it in a non-standard and easy-to-understand form.

Items
Stylized cursor

The most characteristic element of the site; interactive cursor, the shape of which echoes the logo. When you hover on functional elements, the animation is triggered. It serves as a reward for the user’s actions, involves and motivates to explore the site further.

Items
Jump between pages

Animation of transitions between pages creates the feeling that the user always remains on the same page, but one content flies away and another comes to replace it.

Transition animation
Items
Online

Agribusiness Feature & ndash; seasonality. In order to tell visitors about the growth periods of different berries, the timeline became the central theme of the site. It monthly describes the process of ripening and harvesting.

Items
Tables

Website visitors & ndash; potential wholesale buyers of berries. For them it is important to navigate the key characteristics of varieties and storage conditions. All this information was structured and fit into the format of a compact table. More details on the characteristics and useful properties of the berries can be seen on the same page.
